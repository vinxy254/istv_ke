Installation Guide

Android Phone (Google Play)

  1. Open Google Play Store on your Android device
  2. Search for "istv" or use the link above
  3. Tap "Install" and wait for the download to complete
  4. Open istv and start streaming immediately

Android TV (APK)

  1. Download the istv.apk file from the link above
  2. Transfer the file to your Android TV via USB or network
  3. Use a file manager to locate the APK file
  4. Select the file and tap "Install" or "Open"
  5. Grant necessary permissions and enjoy streaming on your big screen

LG WebOS Smart TV

  1. On your LG Smart TV, press the Home button on the remote
  2. Navigate to the LG Content Store
  3. Search for "istv" in the app store
  4. Select istv and click "Install"
  5. Wait for installation to complete and launch the app

Web Browser

  1. Visit https://istvkenya.com on your computer or mobile browser
  2. Click "Watch Live on Web" from the homepage
  3. Select a channel from the available streams
  4. Enjoy free FTA streaming instantly (no installation required)

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection (3G/4G/5G or WiFi)
  • Clear the app cache and try again
  • Make sure your device OS is up to date
  • Contact our support team at support@istvkenya.com
