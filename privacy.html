Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

At istv, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our services.

Data Collection

istv does not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users.

We operate as a viewer-only platform focused solely on streaming content. We do not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
  • Track browsing history or viewing preferences
  • Store user account information or credentials
  • Access your device's personal files, contacts, or media

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes.

Our service operates without:

  • Persistent cookies that track user behavior
  • Third-party analytics or advertising trackers
  • Device fingerprinting or cross-site tracking

Required Permissions

The istv app requires only minimal permissions to function:

  • Internet Permission: Required to stream video content from our servers

We do not request permissions for:

  • Camera or microphone access
  • Location data
  • Contact or calendar information
  • File storage beyond what's needed for streaming
Key Point: The internet permission is exclusively used to enable video streaming. Your device remains completely private while using istv.

Viewer-Only Platform

istv is a viewer-only platform. We do not:

  • Create user accounts or profiles
  • Store watch history or preferences
  • Share your viewing data with third parties
  • Monetize user data through advertising networks

Third-Party Services

istv does not integrate with third-party data collection services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar analytics platforms
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Advertising networks or demand-side platforms

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information for you to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete privacy while using istv.

Content & Legal Compliance

While we prioritize user privacy, we comply with applicable Kenyan laws regarding content distribution and may remove channels if required by law or legitimate copyright claims.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of istv constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@istvkenya.com
Location: Nairobi, Kenya

Bottom Line: Your privacy is paramount. We collect zero personal data, use no tracking cookies, and operate with complete transparency. Stream with confidence.
