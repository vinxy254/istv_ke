About istv

istv is a Free-to-Air content aggregator based in Nairobi, Kenya. We are committed to providing Kenyans with seamless access to live television across all devices— from smartphones to Smart TVs to desktop browsers.

Our mission is to democratize access to Kenyan television and entertainment content, ensuring that geographical location or device type never prevents you from watching what you love.

Whether you're watching live sports, breaking news, or your favorite entertainment channel, istv delivers a premium streaming experience with zero restrictions.
